AI-Powered Customer Retention & Churn Prevention

This demo showcases an AI-powered retention agent for Telekom Srbija. The assistant proactively engages customers identified as being at risk of churn, assesses satisfaction levels, identifies retention drivers and recommends the most appropriate next action.

🎁
